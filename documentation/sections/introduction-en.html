Introduction back to ToC

The employment data are fundamental for socio-economic analysis, territorial planning, and the evaluation of public policies. However, they are often published in heterogeneous formats with little explicit semantics, which hinders their interoperability and reusability. In this context, the W3C Data Cube vocabulary provides an adequate framework for representing multidimensional statistical data in a structured and reusable manner. Moreover, the reuse of consolidated vocabularies and code lists allows for a more precise description of relevant dimensions such as territory, time period, sex, age, or economic activity. The objective of this vocabulary is to define the semantic structure of the employment data cube, specifying the necessary dimensions and measures to represent employment statistics in an interoperable way and in accordance with open standards.
